After more than a year, ArtHop is BACK in downtown Fresno! ArtHop is a community experience hosted by the Fresno Arts Council and featuring street vendors, food trucks, and more than 15 different art galleries and museums open to the public. The event takes place the first Thursday of each month and the first ArtHop since the pandemic began will be held this Thursday, July 1.  Parking will be free of charge at the M Street Arts Complex after 6:00 p.m.

Location: 1419 M Street,  Fresno, CA 93712.

Check out the map below for full details and locations.

Fresno ArtHop Map

We encourage you to visit ArtHop and check out Granville's own M Street Arts Complex to see some amazing artwork from our talented resident artists.
